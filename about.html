About Me

My name is Sanjida Orpi, I am student studying computer science and digital media at NYU Tandon School of Engineering. As a passionate vegetarian for five years, I've experienced the challenges of finding plant-based recipes that offer essential nutrients. I made this website in order to tackle this challenge by curating a diverse collection of delicious and nutritional recipes tailored to plant-based diets. Cooking and learning about cuisine is one of my many passions, and I am enthusiastic about sharing culinary discoveries. Below, you'll find some of my favorite creations. I hope they inspire you on your plant-based journey!
